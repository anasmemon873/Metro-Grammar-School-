Classes

Language Class

The language classes designed just for kids! Through fun activities and games, children explore new words and sounds. Our friendly teachers make learning easy and exciting, helping kids speak confidently

Computer Class

In computer class, children will explore the basics of computers, learning how they work through fun, interactive activities. They will discover the parts of a computer, understand simple coding concepts, and gain hands-on experience with basic software, fostering both their curiosity and foundational tech skills.

Sports Class

In Sports Class, children participate in cricket, football, and basketball, fostering physical fitness and coordination. Regular engagement in these activities promotes overall health and builds strength. Moreover, sports encourage teamwork and confidence, contributing to overall well-being.
